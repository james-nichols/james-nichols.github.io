--- Moi ---
-----------

I'm a postdoctoral research associate at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, UNSW, Sydney Australia. I'm interested in stochastic processes, numerical analysis, fractional & stochastic partial differential equations, high dimensional problems, with particular interest in applying these tools to understand the initial stages of HIV infection. This work is done in collaboration with John Murray and Bruce Henry.

I completed my Ph.D in 2014 with Ian Sloan and Frances Kuo, also at UNSW. I play guitar and rubbish piano. I try to code elegantly. Far too much of my time goes to tinkering with bikes and mobile sound systems.
